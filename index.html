About Budgeto

Budgeto is a smart financial management tool designed for students and individuals living away from home. Our platform allows you to track your income, manage expenses, set budgets, and view insightful reports—all in one place.

Budgeto allows users to securely login, add expenses, categorize them and visualize spending trends using interactive graphs and reports.
